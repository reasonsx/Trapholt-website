ART WORKSHOPS

Nanna Ditzel's Stairscapes blend architectural innovation with aesthetic brilliance. Born from her unique vision, these staircases go beyond utility, becoming sculptural masterpieces that redefine spatial experiences. With meticulous attention to form and material, Ditzel's Stairscapes seamlessly integrate into surroundings, transforming ascent into an artful journey. At Trapholt, you can not only climb these stairscapes but also sit and lie down, making the ordinary act of climbing an exploration of elegance and innovation.

EVENTS FOR EVERYONE

From the playful curves of the Trinidad Chair to the cozy embrace of the Heart Sofa, and the innovative Train Interior Design, each piece tells a unique story of Ditzel's creative evolution. Explore the exhibit, sit back, and absorb the essence of her groundbreaking designs that seamlessly blend comfort, innovation, and timeless style.
