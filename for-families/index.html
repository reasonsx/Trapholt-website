TRAPHOLT IS FOR THE WHOLE FAMILY

COME IN FOR A TREASURE HUNT!

Spot the playful penguin in the painting or the sneaky fox in the corner! Ready to unleash your inner designer? Learning about art is fun! So, dive into the world of modern art and design at Trapholt with our interactive quizzes for families. Embark on a learning adventure with our scavenger hunt challenges or design your very own exhibition! Fun and games await – let's make your museum visit a creative blast!

THERE IS ALWAYS SOMETHING GOING ON

Step into a realm of creativity at Trapholt, where art workshops and captivating sessions await enthusiasts of all ages. Immerse yourself in the collective experience of vibrant events, featuring music concerts and enlightening talks by artists. At Trapholt, everyone is welcome to explore, create, and learn under one roof—a hub for artistic expression, discussions, and boundless creativity in the region.

WE CARE ABOUT YOUR COMFORT

At Trapholt, we warmly welcome families, and entrance is for free for those under 18 . Don't hesitate to bring your little ones along for an art-filled adventure! Enjoy the convenience of borrowing a stroller, find changing tables in our restrooms, and discover a tranquil baby room for a peaceful retreat. We strive to make your visit enjoyable and stress-free, ensuring a comfortable experience for every member of the family.
